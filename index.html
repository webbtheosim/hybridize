Rules

Objective

Build a perfectly aligned, fully paired duplex with the lowest possible final energy.

Setup

  • Two complementary strands are generated.
  • Some bases may begin incorrectly paired (misaligned).
  • Crossing bonds are not allowed (unless enabled in advanced mode).

Each Round

  1. Roll one die for each color (class).
  2. Each color either allows you to Form or Unpair.
  3. You may perform the available actions in any order.

Form

  • Select two complementary, unpaired bases of that color.
  • Bonds may not cross existing bonds.
  • Correctly aligned bonds are straight.
  • Misaligned bonds are curved and may reduce stability.

Unpair

  • Select an existing bond of that color to break it.

Energy Model

  • Each valid bond contributes −1 energy.
  • Each adjacent correctly aligned pair adds an additional −1 stacking bonus.
  • The perfect duplex has energy: −(N + N−1).

Frustration Events

  • If certain dice conditions occur (e.g., doubles), a Frustration Event is triggered.
  • You must add one misaligned bond if possible.

Winning

  • A match ends when no more legal moves remain or the duplex is perfect.
  • Final energy determines the winner.
  • In Single Player mode, minimize final energy across the set.
